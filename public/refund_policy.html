Refund and Return Policy

Effective Date: August 25, 2025

Last Updated: August 26, 2025

At PatientLead Health LLC, we want you to understand how refunds, returns, and cancellations work for all PatientLead+ subscription plans.

Free trials

Eligible plans include a free trial period. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged.

No refunds after billing starts

All subscription payments are non-refundable once the billing period begins.

  • If you cancel in the middle of a billing period, your subscription remains active until the end of the period you paid for.
  • We do not issue partial or prorated refunds for unused time.

Exceptions

We will issue refunds under these circumstances:

  • Duplicate charges caused by billing errors.
  • Incorrect billing amounts due to technical issues.
  • Where required by law, including applicable consumer protection regulations.

How to cancel

You can cancel at any time through your account settings. Your subscription will stay active until the end of the paid period, and no future payments will be collected.

Contact for billing support

For questions about billing or this policy, contact us at subscriptions@patientlead.health.

Acknowledgment

By starting a subscription, you agree to this Refund and Return Policy.

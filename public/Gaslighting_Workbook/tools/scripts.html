When they say: "This sounds like anxiety"

You respond: "I appreciate you considering all possibilities. My mental health is well-managed with my therapist, and these physical symptoms are new and distinct from my anxiety symptoms. What diagnostic tests can we run to rule out physical causes first?"

When they say: "Your tests are normal, so you're fine"

You respond: "I understand the tests are normal, which is good news. However, I'm still experiencing these symptoms daily. What conditions might not show up on these specific tests? What should our next diagnostic steps be?"

When they say: "Let's wait and see"

You respond: "I'd like to establish a concrete timeline. How long should we wait? What specific changes would warrant further investigation? Can we schedule a follow-up now to reassess?"
